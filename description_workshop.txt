[b]This mod adds a dedicated wing slot near the armor and accessories page of the inventory. Updated with permission from original author abluescarab.[/b]

[h1]Usage[/h1]
• Right-click on wings from inventory to move it to wing slot.
• Right-click vanity wings to swap with equipped wings.
• Left-click a set of wings or a dye, then place it in the slot.
• Left-click the eye icon on the equip slot to toggle visibility.
