!!! WARNING !!!
Unequip your wings from the dedicated slot before updating or disabling this mod!

---

This mod adds a dedicated wing slot to the equipment page of the inventory, to the left of the dyes.

* Right-click to equip.
* Shift-right-click to equip in the vanity slot.
* Right-click vanity wings to switch with equipped wings.
* Left-click equipped wings to unequip.
* Left-click the eye icon on the equip slot to toggle visibility.
* Left-click a dye to pick it up, then place it in the dye slot.

[Known Issues]
* Yoraiz0r's Spell eye glow doesn't work
